About

Building in Southern California since 1998

Founded by Kevin Thunder and Brian Jones, Thunder-Jones Contracting Group serves the southern California community with quality construction. TJC prides itself in friendly business practice, safe work zones, and its specialization in tilt-up concrete construction, cast-in-place walls and elevated decks, and slab on grade projects. With a combined 45 years of experience in the San Diego construction industry, Kevin and Brian have created a dynamic team to complete projects on time and above standards.

TJC has an extensive history of performing all types of tilt-up and cast-in-place concrete in virtually every market segment imaginable. Services


Commercial

Our portfolio of projects structures us as a leader in the hospitality industry. Our services include place and finish, formwork, foundations and total package solutions. With our ability to mobilize quickly and go wherever the project demands, our clients recognize our ability to exceed expectation and earn their repeat business..

Multi-Family Residential

Our reputation for meeting the aggressive schedules of this market helps us transform new projects into revenue-generating spaces. We are experienced in providing various levels of service, from foundation packages to total package services. Our intensive knowledge of formwork systems allows for the most cost-effective construction planning, engineering options and design/build services in the market.

Institutional

We have experience in healthcare facilities, including hospitals, laboratories, specialized facilities and nursing homes. With many projects revolving around active facilities, TJC completes each job with a focused dedication to safety, job quality and on-time scheduling. TJC comes prepared to each project to ensure a timely and efficient turn-around for our clients to continue serving our communities.
