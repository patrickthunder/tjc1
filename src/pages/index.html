About

Providing Structural Concrete Construction Expertise to Southern California Since 1998

Thunder Jones is a structural concrete builder based in San Diego County. We specialize in design-build parking structures and apartment/condominium/mulit-use construction. We have worked with various industries including aerospace, pharmaceutical, hospitals, hotels, commercial/retail, and the military. We have established a proven track record of safety, quality and timeliness on our projects. Our employees are trained in the latest of equipment and methods to insure that safety of our employees and others that work around us is our top focus.

Services


Commercial

Our portfolio of projects structures us as a leader in the hospitality industry. Our services include place and finish, formwork, foundations and total package solutions.

Multi-Family Residential

Our reputation for meeting the aggressive schedules of this market helps us transform new projects into revenue-generating spaces. Our intensive knowledge of formwork systems allows for the most cost-effective construction planning, engineering options, and design/build services in the market.

Institutional

We have experience in healthcare facilities, including hospitals, laboratories, specialized facilities and nursing homes. With many projects revolving around active facilities, TJC completes each job with a focused dedication to safety, job quality and on-time scheduling.
