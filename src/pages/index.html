About

Commercial Structural Concrete Expertise in Southern California

Founded in 2000 by Kevin Thunder and Brian Jones, Thunder Jones Contracting Group, Inc. has transformed from a small two-man company to one with over 70 employees. Brian and Kevin have worked closely together in shaping the company and they share the common values of fair practices, moral integrity, and job site safety. They both live in North San Diego County with their families.
