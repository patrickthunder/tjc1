About

Commercial Structural Concrete Expertise in Southern California Since 1998

Founded by Kevin Thunder and Brian Jones, Thunder-Jones Contracting (TJC) Group, Inc. serves Southern California with quality structural concrete construction. TJC prides itself in friendly business practices, safe work zones, and specialization in tilt-up, cast-in-place walls and elevated decks, and slab on grade concrete projects. With a combined 45 years of experience in the San Diego Construction industry, Kevin and Brian have created a dynamic team to complete projects on time and above standards.

TJC Contracting hopes to have an opportunity to work with your company to accomplish your goals.
