About

Providing Structural Concrete Construction Expertise to Southern California Since 1998

Founded by Kevin Thunder and Brian Jones, Thunder-Jones Contracting (TJC) Group, Inc serves the southern California community with quality construction. TJC prides itself in friendly business practices, safe work zones, and specialization in tilt-up concrete construction, cast-in-place walls and elevated decks, and slab on grade projects. With a combined 45 years of experience in the San Diego construction industry, Kevin and Brian have created a dynamic team to complete projects on time and above standards.

The team at TJC Contracting hopes to have an opportunity to work with you to accomplish your goals.
